About us

A little taste of Korea in the heart of Mestre

Welcome to Chingu BBQ. We're a small wok and BBQ place that mixes the Korean food we grew up loving with a few modern touches. If you're after something a bit different tonight, you're in the right spot.

The room is cosy and easy, just right for a night out with friends or a family dinner. Our team will look after you, the portions are generous, the prices are fair, and every dish has clear allergen info on our iPad menus, so you can sit back and enjoy.

  • Generous portions, fair prices
  • BBQ meats with our own sweet sauces
  • Allergen info on every dish
  • Quick, friendly service
